Inches

An inch (abbreviation in) is a unit of measurement equivalent to approximately 1/36th of a yard. It is derived from the word ince from the Old English, which furthermore came from the Latin term uncia, a measurement equivalent to 1/12th of a Roman foot or pes. An inch was defined as 2.54 centimeters ever since 1959 (Encyclopedia Britannica).

Centimeters

One centimeter is equivalent to 1/100thof a meter, or approximately 0.3937 inches (Encyclopedia Britannica).
